CARS-ANTI-CATS Project

A community-driven solution to protect stray cats and ensure safety

About the Project

CAC（CARS-ANTI-CATS) is a community project that combines system design with animal protection efforts. The purpose of CAC system is to prevent stray cats from entering vehicle parts for warmth, avoiding injury or death.

About How it works Community Impact

How It Works

The system is powered by car batteries and features infrared sensors for precise detection. Ultrasonic emitters were calibrated after extensive testing to ensure the safety and comfort of the cats.

Community Engagement

Beyond the technical solution, this project involved active collaboration with the local community. Through presentations in residential groups and discussions with frequent feeders of stray cats, I garnered support and ideas. With permission from property management, insulated shelters were placed in approved locations. This initiative has brought together people who share a common goal of improving the lives of stray cats while also solving urban street problems.
