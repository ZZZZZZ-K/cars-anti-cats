CARS-ANTI-CATS Project

A community-driven solution to protect stray cats and ensure safety

About the Project

CAC（CARS-ANTI-CATS) is a community project that combined system design with animal protection means. The purpose of CAC system is to prevent stray cats from accidentally entering parts of a vehicle’s structure, particularly for warmth, which could result in injury or death. Additionally, it aims to avoid potential operational malfunctions caused by such incidents within the vehicle..

How It Works

The system is powered by car batteries and features infrared sensors for precise detection. Ultrasonic emitters were calibrated after extensive testing to ensure the safety and comfort of the cats, balancing effectiveness and welfare.

Community Engagement

Beyond the technical solution, this project involved active collaboration with the local community. Through presentations in residential groups and discussions with local shelters, we were able to implement the system and offer adoption opportunities for the cats. More than 80% of cats found forever homes.

Impact and Future Directions

The impact of this project is far-reaching. It has not only helped improve the safety and well-being of stray cats, but also fostered a greater sense of community responsibility and awareness. With future iterations, we hope to expand the reach of the system and continue making a positive difference for animals in need.
