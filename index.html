CARS-ANTI-CATS Project

A community-driven solution to protect stray cats and ensure safety

About the Project

CAC（CARS-ANTI-CATS) is a community project that combined system design with animal protection means. The purpose of CAC system is to prevent stray cats from accidentally entering parts of a vehicle’s structure, particularly for warmth, which could result in injury or death. Additionally, it aims to avoid potential operational malfunctions caused by such incidents within the vehicle.

In the winter of 2022, I noticed a concerning issue in my community: stray cats were frequently seeking warmth under cars and inside engine compartments. Many residents reported seeing cats running around the underground parking garage, and some even thought they had accidentally hit a cat. As a member of both the automotive club and the animal protection society at school, I was motivated to find a solution to this problem. How It Works

Developed a system based on Raspberry Pi 4 that detects the presence of a cat near a vehicle using infrared (IR) sensors, and triggers an ultrasonic sound emitter to deter the cat. The system should increase the sound intensity if multiple sensors detect the cat simultaneously.

Raspberry Pi 4: The central control unit that processes inputs from the infrared sensors and controls the ultrasonic emitter.

Infrared Sensors (7 units): Positioned around the vehicle to detect motion. Each sensor outputs a HIGH signal when a cat is detected and LOW when not.

Ultrasonic Sound Emitter: Emits ultrasonic sound when activated. The intensity of the sound increases based on the number of sensors detecting the cat.

Battery supply: The system draws power from the vehicle’s 12V battery though a step-down module (DC-DC). The system is always active when the vehicle is parked and running.

Impact

This project has not only raised awareness about the stray cat issue but has also contributed to preventing accidental harm to animals. Many vehicle owners and drivers have reported positive feedback after using the system in place, ensuring that the cats are kept safe and cars are maintained without unexpected interruptions.

Community Impact

Through the success of CAC system, we have involved the local community in a more direct way. It has helped spread awareness about responsible car ownership and animal welfare.
